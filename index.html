About SellScript

SellScript is a PHP script to sell your digital content direct.
Games, music, movies, ebooks — if it's a digital file, you can sell it with SellScript.

No middlemen. Sell direct to your customers using Stripe, PayPal, Paymill & Coinbase, with more payment options on the way.

Manage your orders without jumping through hoops. SellScript comes with a complete administration site, where you can manage orders and change settings quickly and easily.

Simple and easy to set up. We've made sure SellScript is as easy to set up as other web software.
